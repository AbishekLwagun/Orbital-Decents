# Orbital Descent

**Orbital Descent** is a high-tension space simulation game set in the year 2147. Developed as a student project, the game puts the player in the role of an astronaut stationed alone inside a research satellite orbiting an unknown planet. The mission: study the planet's gravity, atmosphere, and surface conditions to determine if it is safe for future human exploration.

## 🌌 The Story
* **Setting:** The year is 2147.
* **The Mission:** Perform routine maintenance and monitor gravity readings while stationed in a research satellite.
* **The Conflict:** As the satellite moves closer to the planet, unstable gravitational waves damage internal systems, causing objects to drift faster and modules to fail.

## 🎮 Gameplay & Mechanics
Instead of combat, **Orbital Descent** focuses on environmental puzzles and survival through system repair.

* **Dynamic Gravity:** Movement shifts from zero-gravity floating to "heavy" movement as the planet's gravitational pull increases.
* **Puzzle System:** Players progress by repairing damaged systems using logic, observation, and simple repair mechanics.
* **Environment:** The game takes place in one main environment featuring a Control Room, Corridor, and Engineering/Thruster Room.

### Game Stages
1. **Stage 1 - Normal Operation:** Introduction to basic controls and environment with low gravity and no time pressure.
2. **Stage 2 - Gravity Instability:** Gravity gradually increases, movement becomes heavier, and emergency lighting activates.
3. **Stage 3 - Emergency Repair:** A major surge damages the thruster system; the player must reach the thruster room and complete a timed repair to prevent a crash.
